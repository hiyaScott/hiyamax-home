A collection of hand-built ceramic vessels shaped like small robots, painted in stark black-and-white with touches of red and blue. One figure stands tall with a square head and watchful eyes, while two rounded companions sit nearby—one labeled LOGIC in calm blue, the other MAGIC in playful purple.

Turn them over and a different message appears. Inside each hollow base, painted in bold strokes: PROTECT, SURVIVE, OBEY. The words sit quietly beneath the surface, as if the vessels themselves carry hidden instructions, a playful nod to the unspoken rules that govern both machines and imagination.
