A trio of small canvases hung against a warm brick wall, each capturing a distinct mood through bold acrylic strokes. The first piece balances cool greys and blues with flashes of coral warmth. The second explores layered greens and earth tones suggesting distant landscapes. The third dissolves into soft pinks, lavenders, and sky blues—an airy, dreamlike composition.

Together they form a quiet study in contrast: restrained versus vibrant, structured versus loose, grounded versus floating.
